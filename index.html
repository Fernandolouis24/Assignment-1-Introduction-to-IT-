Project Idea

Overview and Motivation

My project will be about an application that help people find a local tour guide to help them explore a specific area, whether it’s a popular tourist area or remote and rarely known area. Before covid happened, I like to travel to another city in Indonesia or travelling to another country, and this idea come from my concern every time I travel or move to a new country. Since I arrive here in Melbourne, I just walk around the city area, but I really want to explore every café, restaurant, activities that I can try, and interesting places in the suburbs as well. However, the recommendations on the internet mostly just in the cbd area, and that is why this app can help people who want to explore and find out nice place which has not been posted on the internet (hidden gem) with the help of a local guide. This application will also provide benefits for both parties because people who live in the local area get a new job, whereas people who use the service can get a new experience and get recommendations from a local about good restaurants or activities to do in that area. It will be accessible on both Android and IOS to make it easier for people to access it anywhere and anytime.

Description

After finished installing this application on the user’s device, users will be asked to create a new account by registering an email address followed by filling in the required information such as address, phone number, interest, and entering identification (passport, id) for security reasons. After completing the personal data, the user can now choose wheter he wants to join and become a local tour guide for the application or seeking for tour guide in any particular area. For people who want to become a local tour guides, they will have to submit in more data like a photo, description about themselves, location they want to work, and their rate. On the other hand, people who look for a tour guide can directly jump in to the map page and search for a specific area that they want to explore. Afterwards, a list of tour guide in the area will come out complete with their hobby, interest and details of the schedule about the places they will visit and also the estimated time of the tour and the price (individual/groups), and after both parties agree, meeting point and time will come out and the arrival time of the tour guide will be more early. Finally, after the tour is finished, the user can give a tip, rating, and comment about their experience with the tour guide and how they can improve their services. Through this ratings and comments, other users can be helped when choosing a tour guide, it is also can helped and improve the rating in the tour guide profile.

These are the main features in this application:

  • Sign up and login
  • Calender with appointment slot
  • Push notifications
  • Multi-language support
  • Booking history
  • Estimator tour cost
  • Live tracking
